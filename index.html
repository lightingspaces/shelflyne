Shelflyne is the next evolution in retail shelving systems. It has been designed totally wire free to allow free movement of shelves which light up automatically when locked in place.

The patented modular system has been designed by shopfitters for shopfitters. Instalation is super simple, requiring significantly less time and hassle than traditional retail illuminated shelving systems.
